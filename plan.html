Mission Statement

Our goals at CossiePlay is to increase the number of people involved with and aware of Cosplay, to include more young people in parties and to increase our sales of homemade and second hand costumes.

Success Evaluation

The mission statement will be considered a success if the average age of a Cosplay party goer becomes anywhere between 13-30; if the community becomes more aware of Cosplay, whether that be through expressions of interest, questions or more visitors to parties; and if sales of both homemade and second-hand costumes increases.

Target Audience

There are two target audiences mentioned in the Client Content & Information; however one is more clearly stressed than the other. The first is the community as a whole; this Target Audience is not the primary objective. The second is young people, specifically high school kids and young adults. This Target Audience appears to be the main objective of CossiePlay, and so the website will be created accordingly.

To appeal to a younger audience, the website should be eye catching, both in the site’s information architecture and in its colour scheme, pictures etc. It should have helpful parts such as the “no more than three clicks” rule applied to links, or a click to the top of page button. Website does not need to cater for users that will get easily lost, and time used for this will be spared.
